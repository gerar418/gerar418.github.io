Fernando Piquer

Fernando Piquer is the founder and CEO of the Madrid-based eSports team Movistar Riders. For over twenty years, his professional career has been linked to the Internet and digital entertainment. Before the creation of the eSports club, he founded Bitoon Games, the first Spanish free-to-play gaming company. Previously, he was a founding partner of Zinkia, where he participated in the creation and exploitation of the animated series Pocoyo and served as director of the company's video game division for over five years. Throughout his career, he has worked on designing digital strategies and products for brands such as Coca-Cola, BBVA, Vodafone, and Endesa, among others.
